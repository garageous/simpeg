Lean Software Development

Inspired by Lean Manufacturing in Toyota Production System

(TPS), Lean principles aim to improve efficiency by minimizing

waste during building products.

Waste is, anything that does not transform your products and

services into what the customer needs. Anything that does not

create value for your customer.

Kanban Framework

The Kanban framework is one of the manifestations of Lean & Agile

Principles. Kanban originates from the Japanese word billboard or signpost.

Kanban is based on visual cues and serves to improve efficiency and

performance.

Continuous Improvement

4

There is no one size fits all process. Even after using popular frameworks,

there is always space to improve the process with your team.

Ask yourself:

1. Does the process run smoothly as your team expected

2. If yes, what could be better? Can we decrease lead time and cycle

team further?

3. If not, what doesnt work?

Last but not least, always test your new process and monitor which one works

best for your team.
